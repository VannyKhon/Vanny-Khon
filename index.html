I want to live in a community where people are open and kind, where we share food and thought. Personally, I love music, classic literature, poetry, satire, and mathematics. I completed my graduate work in applied mathematics at Boston University and now work on a small organic farm where I grow fruit, vegetables, and flowers to share with my community. I also teach math, science, and English at a local middle school with a curriculum tailored to the local context. I use my farm as an observatory to study insects and their biodiversity through soundscapes and fieldwork. In my free time, I am building a benchmark to measure AI capability and safety in the Khmer language.
